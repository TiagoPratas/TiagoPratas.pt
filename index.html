The story

The designer Tiago Pratas, currently working and living in Lisbon (Portugal), is mainly active in the web/mobile interfaces design field, dealing with projects for public and private clients. In 2010 he founded the ComunicaTe Web Design & Branding company in Coimbra, the city where he was born in 1988.
